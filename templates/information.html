Understanding the Goals

Welcome to GIC's Data Collection Initiative

We are committed to creating a learning atmosphere that enables students to reach their full potential both academically and personally. We have put in place a thorough data collection program with the aim of gaining insights into the academic progress of our students in order to accomplish this goal.

Why Do We Collect Data on Student Progress?

We are devoted to customizing academic help to each individual student in order to suit their specific needs. By using this data, we can improve our support initiatives and make sure that all students get the help they require to succeed academically. and assessing the efficiency of instructional strategies is made easier when we comprehend how students interact with their curriculum.

How Does Your Contribution Make a Difference?

Your Voice Matters.
Your active involvement in the process of data acquisition is a fundamental component of our dedication to providing education that is centered around the needs and interests of students.

Your Privacy and Security

We know how important it is for your info to be safe and private. You can be sure that all the information we collect is kept secret and follows all the rules for data security. Your personal information is hidden, and only thoughts that have been collected are used for analysis.
